Welcome to BAPS Wallpaper ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our wallpaper application (the "App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and want to make sure you understand how your data is collected and used.

We use the collected information to improve and personalize your experience with the App, including optimizing ad content and frequency.
